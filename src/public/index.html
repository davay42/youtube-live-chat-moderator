Youtube Chat Moderator

By: Praveen
  1. Authorize the bot to access your account and wait for the response
  2. Click Get Active Chat this will get the first active live stream chat from your account
  3. To start listening to the chat click the Start Tracking CHat button and wait for the response
  4. To stop tracking the chat click the Stop tracking chat button
  5. Insert message button adds a Hello everyone msg in the live chat
